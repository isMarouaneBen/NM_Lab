Welcome to NMLab

NMLab simplifies healthcare management for a seamless medical experience.

NMLab is a web application designed for efficient healthcare management. It features dedicated interfaces for doctors, patients, and administrators. Doctors can manage appointments, access medical records, and provide prescriptions, while patients can book consultations and track their health data. Administrators oversee system operations to ensure smooth workflow.
